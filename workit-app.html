Work and Things
WorkIt is a beautifully crafted workspace designed to help you stay focused, organized, and connected to everything your work needs.
Built around a unique system of Cards and Things, WorkIt transforms complex projects into clear, manageable flows, whether you’re planning a renovation, preparing a creative project, organizing a trip, planning your next content creation, planning that party you always wanted to host, planning your wedding or managing everyday tasks.
Cards
Focused units of work that break your projects into actionable, trackable steps.
Things
The products, tools, materials, ideas, references, and resources connected to your work.
Workflow
WorkIt is built around fast, repeatable reviews that make it effortless to revisit your work, update progress, and maintain momentum throughout the day. This way each card moves through clear stages To Do, In Progress, Done, or Won't Do. Making it easy to review and update progress anytime on review.
Also, the card deck-based review offers simple swipe gestures:
  • Swipe right to instantly complete a card
  • Swipe left to continue reviewing the next task
While the primary buttons keep up with proper ToDo to In Progress to Done flow
Review Summary
At the end of each review session, WorkIt presents a clear progress summary, encouraging consistent check-ins and helping you stay connected to your goals over time.
A More Visual Way to Work
Attach tools, materials, inspirations, or real-world objects directly to your work and create a workspace that feels visual, personal, and deeply connected to the things that matter.
WorkIt uses advanced Apple Vision image analysis to intelligently isolate objects from photos and transform them into beautiful stickers.
Use properties attribution to describe items more precisely. Supports length, weight, volume, size, dimensions, radius/circumference, and color.
